Housing in Massachusetts Opportunity Zones

Percentage of Renters

Most opportunity zones have a high percentage of renters.

Housing Starts

Most housing production is clustered around a comparable household size per community, but a handful of municipalities have hot housing markets building more than their weight. There is a risk that opportunity zones accelerate these trends. There are also some cities like Worcester not building enough, so perhaps the designation will help encourage production.
